All people wishing to be tested myst have a valid phone number to be notified of results by text or phone call.Testing dates, times and locations are as follows:Wednesday June 24th Mineola Civic Center (1150 North Newsome - click here for map) 9:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.This is a Drive-Thru Test, meaning you will remain in your vehicle while having samples taken with a nasal swabYou will only be screened if you are showing at least one symptom of having COVID-19First responders can be tested but will need to state they are a first responder when you register, regardless of whether you have any symptom of COVID-19Pre-registration is required! You can register by calling (512) 883 - 2400 or register online at TxCOVIDTest.orgThursday June 25th Henderson Civic Center (1500 Lake Forest Parkway - click here for map) 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.This is a Walk-In Test, meaning you will need to wear a mask before entry and while inside the facilityAny non-symptomatic person from any county can come to receive a free COVID-19 test and no appointment is necessary.No registration is necessary. You can arrive anytime between 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.Friday June 26thHenderson Civic Center (1500 Lake Forest Parkway - click here for map) 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.This is a Walk-In Test, meaning you will need to wear a mask before entry and while inside the facilityAny non-symptomatic person from any county can come to receive a free COVID-19 test and no appointment is necessary.No registration is necessary. You can arrive anytime between 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.Saturday June 27th Quitman Fire Station (413 East Goode - click here for map) 9:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.This is a Drive-Thru Test, meaning you will remain in your vehicle while having samples taken with a nasal swabYou will only be screened if you are showing at least one symptom of having COVID-19First responders can be tested but will need to state they are a first responder when you register, regardless of whether you have any symptom of COVID-19Pre-registration is required! You can register by calling (512) 883 - 2400 or register online at TxCOVIDTest.orgSaturday June 27th Grand Saline Chamber of Commerce (203 East Pacific Street - click here for map) 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.This is a Walk-In Test, meaning you will need to wear a mask before entry and while inside the facilityAny non-symptomatic person from any county can come to receive a free COVID-19 test and no appointment is necessary.No registration is necessary. You can arrive anytime between 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.Sunday June 28thWills Point Community Room (307 North 4th Street - click here for map) 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.This is a Walk-In Test, meaning you will need to wear a mask before entry and while inside the facilityAny non-symptomatic person from any county can come to receive a free COVID-19 test and no appointment is necessary.No registration is necessary.
