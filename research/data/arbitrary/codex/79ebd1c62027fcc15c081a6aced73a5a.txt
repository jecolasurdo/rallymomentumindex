New transfer student, Mikoto Aiura, joins Kusuo's class. She is a physic and is looking for the mysterious S.K., who is apparently her soulmate. Escape the Aural Sight! Kusuo skips a day of school to see the extent of Mikoto's abilities, she is able to figure out the personalities of nearly everyone through their auras. The Circumstances of Delicate Children Yuuta is getting bullied and Kusuo helps him out, only to find out everything is a huge misunderstanding. Another Time Leap Challenge! Time keeps repeating itself every few seconds and Kusuo tries to make time go forward by thinking absolutely nothing. At one point, it becomes uneasy as a child and Riki get into danger in one of the loops.
