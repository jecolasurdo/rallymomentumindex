These are external links and will open in a new window Close share panel Image caption Police were called to the scene in Barford Suspected human bones have been discovered next to a river in a Warwickshire village.Police are investigating after the remains were found near a bridge in Barford at 18:30 BST on Tuesday. Divers have also been at the scene.A Warwickshire Police spokesman said: "An investigation is in its very early stages and all lines of inquiry are being explored."A scene guard will remain in the area whilst inquiries are being conducted."
