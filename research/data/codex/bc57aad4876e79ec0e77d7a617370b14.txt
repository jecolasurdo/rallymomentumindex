Updated: 10:50 AM CDT May 31, 2020 WVTM 13 Digital Update:Hoover Police arrested twenty people Saturday night on charges ranging from blocking traffic, disorderly conduct, criminal trespassing, resisting arrest, and one person was charged with carrying a firearm during a demonstration. Windows of Jared Jewelry and Von Maur stores were damaged.No injuries were reported.The police department issued this statement:-------------A large group of protesters gathered in Hoover Saturday night.Hoover Police officers were called to Highway 31 near Hoover City Hall, as a large crowd of people holding signs were on both sides of the highway.WVTM 13 News crews saw at least four people arrested and placed in a transport van.Our crew heard the crowd chanting "No justice, no peace".The demonstration comes in the wake of the death of George Floyd during a police arrest in Minneapolis.Also, people remembered E.J. Bradford Jr., who was shot and killed by a Hoover Police officer inside the Riverchase Galleria in November 2018.
