A few hundred people showed up at a local rally responding to these events Saturday in Springfield. They held signs, chanted, and some shared thoughts and personal experiences during a rally. Attendees say they want to promote peace and stop violence. The event was organized by several high school students who say they wanted to create a peaceful place and time for people to express their desire for change.
