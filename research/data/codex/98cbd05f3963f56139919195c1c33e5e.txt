RICARDO TORRES Facebook Twitter WhatsApp SMS Email Print Save RACINE — A group of more than 50 people have been holding a protest in front of the Racine County Law Enforcement Center (LEC) regarding the recent Racine police shooting of Donte Shannon.The group marched from 14th Street and Park Avenue to the LEC in response to a police shooting of Donte D. Shannon on Jan. 17 on the 1400 block of Park Avenue. Shannon, 26, was fatally shot by two Racine police officers that afternoon while, reportedly, fleeing a traffic stop. The Wisconsin Department of Justice is investigating the shooting by police officers Chad Stillman, a 15-year veteran, and Peter Boeck, a 16-year veteran.There is another protest scheduled for 3 p.m. The Journal Times will update this story.
