"We don't tolerate excessive use of force,” Mercer said at the gathering in front of a giant whale sculpture on the city's waterfront. A group sang Alaska Native songs, and most people practiced social distancing and wore masks.Gloria Merry helped promote the low-key vigil to show support for people grieving in Minneapolis and elsewhere, and bring a local focus to a national event."What goes on outside of Juneau still affects us in Juneau,” she said.
