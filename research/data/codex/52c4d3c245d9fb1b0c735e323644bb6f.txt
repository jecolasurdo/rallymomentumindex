The rebuilding was one of several events marking the anniversary.Photo by Christian Gooden, cgooden@post-dispatch.com Christian Gooden Fourth anniversary of Ferguson police shooting of Michael Brown Jr. Melanie Randels of Ferguson adds a teddy bear to a newly-formed memorial on Wednesday, Aug. 8, 2018, at the placard on Canfield Drive commemorating Michael Brown Jr., the 18-year-old shot to death by a Ferguson police officer four years ago. Thursday is the fourth anniversary of his death. The rebuilding of the memorial was one of several events marking the date.
