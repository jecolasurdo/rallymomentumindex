"Black Lives Matter Cincinnati" organized the rally Friday night. More than 250 people turned out for the protest on the courthouse steps, calling for justice for Sam Dubose. His death on July 19 came amid months of national scrutiny of police dealings with African-Americans. Police say they arrested at least six people during Friday night's rally. Former University of Cincinnati police officer Ray Tensing has pleaded not guilty to murder and voluntary manslaughter in the shooting.
