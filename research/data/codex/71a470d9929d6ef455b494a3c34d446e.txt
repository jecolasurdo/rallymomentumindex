Cone Silence Dr. Sendil Krishnan and about 100 other Cone Health medical professions take a knee for nine minutes outside Mose Cone Hospital in Greensboro, N.C., on Friday, June 5, 2020. Health professionals with White Coats for Black Lives across the country demonstrated their support for Black Lives Matter. The organization was formed in 2014 after the death of Eric Garner in New York City borough of Staten Island. Krishnan said its goal is to identify racism in medicine and eliminate it.
