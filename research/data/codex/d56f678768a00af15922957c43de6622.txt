Facebook Live—Colorado Springs City Council Facebook page @coscitycouncil Dial in: +1 720-617-3426, Meeting number: 156 778 281# How to participate The public can email general comments or comments regarding the agenda in advance of a meeting to: AllCouncil@coloradosprings.gov Comments will also be taken through Facebook Live during the meeting During the April 28, 2020 meeting, you may provide comments by calling +1 720-617-3426 United States, Denver (Toll) and entering this Conference ID: 156 778 281#, Please enter *6 to unmute your line to comment when called to speak. Regular meetings occur on the 2nd and 4th Tuesday of the month.
