MODESTO, Calif. — About 15 people were arrested in Modesto during the George Floyd protests, according to the Modesto Police Department. Hundreds of demonstrators marched through the area to protest the police custody death of Floyd. Advertisement Protesters made their way into downtown Modesto, police said.
