Dozens of people marched into the streets carrying signs and shouting "Hey hey! Ho ho! These racist cops have got to go!" and "We are done with racist police!" Demonstrators passed the city hall and ended at Carroll Creek. At the bridge, marchers paused to share stories about discrimination, according to the Frederick News-Post. Frederick police said officers were on the scene to re-route traffic. No protesters were arrested.
