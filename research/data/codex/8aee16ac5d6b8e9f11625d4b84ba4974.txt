A movement focused on police brutality against African Americans. Today there was an emphasis on remembering George Floyd, the Minnesota man who died in police custody last month. A message that was repeated while on the steps of the Capitol was the power of voting. The most substantial messages were the ones chanted on the streets of Augusta.
