Syracuse University Black Lives Matter activists hold flash protest on promenade - syracuse.comSkip to ArticleSyracuse University Black Lives Matter activists hold flash protest on promenadeUpdated Mar 22, 2019; Posted Oct 06, 2016 By Julie McMahon | jmcmahon@syracuse.com SYRACUSE, N.Y. -- At Syracuse University Wednesday, about 200 students lined the University Place Promenade. At once, they dropped to the ground.Exactly 10 minutes later, the students stood up and walked away.The silent protest was to raise awareness about the 102 unarmed black Americans killed by police in 2015. Demonstrators wore all black with the names of those individuals on their chests.The "die-in" was a surprise protest.
