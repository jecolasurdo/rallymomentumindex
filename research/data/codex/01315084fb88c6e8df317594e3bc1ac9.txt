Hundreds gathered in Old City outside the apartment building where Mayor Jim Kenney resides to call for cutting funding to police in favor of neighborhood services. South Philadelphia residents gathered to protect a Christopher Columbus statue. A Columbus statue in Camden was taken down, but residents who support its removal have taken possession of its pieces in an ongoing protest. In Delaware, Wilmington officials are removing statues of Columbus and Caesar Rodney.
