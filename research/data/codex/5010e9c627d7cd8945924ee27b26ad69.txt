SACRAMENTO, Calif. (AP) — Law enforcement closed the State Capitol an hour early Friday in expectation of a protest of police killings of black people one day after five police officers were killed at a similar rally in Dallas Thursday.However, this protest remained peaceful as hundreds marched multiple times around the Capitol, focusing their chants at law enforcement as they asked for peace and justice.
