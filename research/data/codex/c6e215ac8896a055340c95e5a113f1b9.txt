Please look at the time stamp on the story to see when it was last updated. × Black Lives Matter protest marches to Safeco Field SEATTLE – A Black Lives Matter protest in Seattle turned into a march shutting down streets from Westlake Park to Safeco Field on Saturday night. The gathering began at Westlake Park in downtown Seattle just after 6pm. More than 600 protesters then took to the streets, starting with intersection at 4th and Pine and walking towards Safeco Field. The protest remained peaceful and ended where it began at 10pm in Westlake.
