The event lasted for 8 minutes and 46 seconds, the amount of time that a Minneapolis police officer pressed his knee onto George Floyd's neck.Related: Thousands protest at Boston's Franklin Park Tuesday nightParticipants then headed into Franklin Park for a vigil.Protesters were still taking to the streets of Boston, as well as in other areas — including Brockton — after planned demonstrations had ended.Globe reporters are on the scene.
