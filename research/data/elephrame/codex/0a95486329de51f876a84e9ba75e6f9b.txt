They protested against police brutality following the police killing of 46-year-old George Floyd, of Minneapolis, on Memorial Day. Floyd's death and the history of police violence against black people has sparked protests around the world. The Newburgh protest was peaceful, but not silent, as dozens of people stepped forward to share ideas for change and their experiences with racism and police while protesters stood in a circle.
