Travel Things To Do at the Shore Food Health Real Estate Black Lives Matter protesters call for Seth Williams to resign by RealTime Desk, Posted: March 27, 2017 A small group of Black Lives Matter protesters briefly blocked traffic around City Hall Monday morning during a demonstration to call for the resignation of District Attorney Seth Williams. No arrests were made during the protest on the south side of City Hall in front of the District Attorney's Office at 3 South Penn Square. Related stories Inquirer Editorial: Williams shouldn't wait another day to resign For Seth Williams, a push for reform undone by penchant for the high life Three questions, after a week of lowlights for Pa. politicians The handful of protesters were led by Asa Khalif, a Black Lives Matter organizer. Williams has resisted calls to resign since his indictment last week on federal corruptions charges.
