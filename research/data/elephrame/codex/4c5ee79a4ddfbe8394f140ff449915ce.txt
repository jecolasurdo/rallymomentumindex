Anti-racism activists to stage demonstration in Warsaw June 6 Anti-racism activists to stage demonstration outside US Embassy in Warsaw on June 6; additional demonstrations planned in the capital, avoid all protests TIMEFRAME expected from 6/5/2020, 12:00 AM until 6/7/2020, 11:59 PM (Europe/Warsaw). COUNTRY/REGION Warsaw Warning Security Poland EventThe US Embassy in Warsaw has issued a demonstration alert ahead of a protest organized by the Union Against Racism to be held in the capital on Saturday, June 6. Participants are expected to gather between 15:00 and 16:00 (local time) in front of the embassy. The issued alert indicates that various demonstrations have been planned across the capital on Saturday, between the hours of 12:00 and 20:45. Localized disruptions and a heightened security presence are to be expected. AdviceIndividuals in Warsaw are advised to monitor the situation, avoid all protests and demonstrations as a precaution, and abide by all restrictions implemented by local authorities.
