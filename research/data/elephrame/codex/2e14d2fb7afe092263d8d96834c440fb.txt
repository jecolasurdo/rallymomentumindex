(KCRA) — A die-in protest was held Monday at the UC Davis School of Medicine in Sacramento over the deadly police shooting of Stephon Clark. The Sacramento County District Attorney's Office announced earlier this month that it would not seek criminal charges against the officers who shot and killed Clark.
