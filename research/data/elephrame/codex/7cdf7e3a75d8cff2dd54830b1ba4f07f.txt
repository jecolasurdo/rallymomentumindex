(Dilip Vishwanat/Getty Images)By Soraya Nadia McDonald closeSoraya Nadia McDonaldBioBioOctober 14, 2014 at 1:23 AM EDTThey're everywhere.After making a surprise appearance at the St. Louis Symphony, where they interrupted the night with a performance of "Requiem for Mike Brown,” protesters demanding justice in the killing of unarmed teenager Michael Brown made another highly public stand at Monday night's game between the St. Louis Rams and the San Francisco 49ers.Support our journalism. Subscribe today.arrow-rightThe protests during ESPN's Monday Night Football broadcast were part of a wider call to action, broadly recognized as Ferguson October, as the public awaits an announcement regarding whether or not officer Darren Wilson will be charged in Brown's death. Wilson shot and killed Brown Aug. 9 and has remained on paid leave from his job with the Ferguson police department.
