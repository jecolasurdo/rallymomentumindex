Mayor Suthers will highlight infrastructure progress, along with public safety improvements, economic development and other accomplishments over the past year. The Mayor will also identify some of the city's current challenges. The State of the City address is hosted by the Colorado Springs Chamber and EDC. The Mayor's speech will be broadcast live on Facebook @CityofCOS and @MayorofCOS starting at approximately 12:30 p.m. It will also air on the city's government access station, SpringsTV, found on Comcast Channel 18 and 880 (HD) and Century Link Channel 18.
